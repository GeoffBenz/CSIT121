Grandma's Christmas Sugar Cookies

Geoffrey Benecewicz
8/30/23

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups flour
  • 1 t baking soda
  • 1/2 t baking powder
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 cup softened butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 t vanilla extract

Recipe Steps:

  1. preheat oven to 375 degrees F
  2. in one bowl mix the flour,baking soda,and baking powder together
  3. mix the sugar and butter together untill smooth
  4. beat the egg and add it to the sugar with the vanilla
  5. mix the 2 bowls together till the dough is formed
  6. place 1 to 2 inch bals of dough 2 inches apart on an ungreased backing sheets
  7. bake for 8 to 10 minutes then let cool on a wire rack

As a kid I would bake these cookies with my grandma at christmas time. now every holiday season I bake these delicious sugar cookies in her memory.
